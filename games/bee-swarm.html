How to Play

Click the flower field to collect pollen. Your bees also collect pollen automatically! Convert pollen into honey, then spend honey on eggs to hatch new bees, upgrades, and unlocking new fields. Earn Tickets by hatching rare bees! Spend tickets on powerful special eggs. Keyboard: 1-8 switch fields, C to convert, S to save.
